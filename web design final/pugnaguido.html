Upsilon

Our flagship game will be a survival sandbox taking place over a whole solar system.
You explore a fully dynamic solar system with high fidelity physics simulated at every point, from full orbital mechanics, to realistic re-entry and flight.
You will find AI driven factions throughout the system that have dynamic relations with you and each other.
You can have great space battles or underwater fights with monsters, travel to different planets like water planets or jungle planets.
The possibilities are limitless with a procedurally generated solar system and detailed planets.
Experience aero, hydro dynamics and advanced orbital mechanics, all while creating you're own cars, ships, planes and other transportation devices to get around.
